7th Indian School on Natural Language Processing and its Applications

Natural Language Processing

November 26-30, 2020
Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur

On behalf of the Association for Logic in India (ALI), Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST), Shibpur announces the seventh edition of Indian School on Logic and its Applications (ISLA). The theme of the school of this edition is ''Natural Language Processing''. The school will be held in collaboration with Calcutta Logic Circle from November 26 to November 30, 2020. The venue is the Main Computer Lab, Department of Information Technology, IIEST, Shibpur.

The ISLA is a reputed school, organized in different parts of India. It is a biennial event of ALI. The past editions of the school were held in Jadavpur University, PSG College of Technology, IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, Manipal University, and Tezpur University. The objective is to present before graduate students and researchers of the country, some basics as well as active research areas in logic. The School typically attracts students and teachers from mathematics, philosophy and computer science departments.

The school will target to cover among others the following topics:

1. Foundations of NLP
2. Algebraic Logic
3. Python for NLP
4. Word Embedding
5. Philosophy of Computer Science
6. Language Modelling
7. Programming Approach to Logical Concepts in Computer Science
8. Information Retrieval and Applications
