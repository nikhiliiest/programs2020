Travel and Accommodation

How To Reach IIEST, Shibpur :

Shibpur is well connected to Kolkata by Road. IIEST, Shibpur is at a distance of 25 kms from the Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport, Kolkata. From Kolkata Airport you can take a taxi from the Airport taxi stand to IIEST, Shibpur (Howrah). The travel time is approximately 1 hr 30 mins.

Howrah Railway junction is well connected to most of the major cities of India by rail. IIEST is at a distance of around 6 kms from Howrah railway station and is about 20 mins drive. You can avail taxi services to reach IIEST from Howrah Railway station.

Accommodation :

Accommodation will be provided. Outstation participants are requested to book their accommodation at the earliest. Expenses are included in the program fees. For any assistanship about accommodation, email to us - sukant@it.iiests.ac.in or mihiriiest@gmail.com.
